## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

**Sign Up:**
1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your email for verification link
4. Click the link to verify your account
5. Complete your profile with username and bio

**Profile Setup:**
- Upload a profile picture (recommended)
- Add a bio describing your music style
- Link your social media accounts
- Set your notification preferences

## For Buyers: Finding & Purchasing Hooks

### Discovering Content

**Browse Hooks:**
- Visit the Discover page to see all available hooks
- Use filters to narrow by genre, price, BPM, or key
- Sort by newest, popular, or price
- Click on any hook to hear a preview

**Search:**
- Use the search bar to find specific styles or keywords
- Search by producer name, genre, or mood
- Save your favorite searches for quick access

**Playlists:**
- Browse curated playlists by genre or mood
- Create your own playlists of favorites
- Follow other users' playlists

### Making a Purchase

**Buying a Hook:**
1. Click on a hook to view details
2. Choose your license type:
   - **Basic**: Personal use, non-commercial projects
   - **Premium**: Commercial use, unlimited streams
   - **Exclusive**: Full rights, remove from marketplace
3. Click "Purchase License"
4. Enter payment information (Stripe secure checkout)
5. Receive instant download link

**After Purchase:**
- Download your hook (WAV/MP3 formats)
- Access your license certificate
- View purchase history in your profile
- Contact seller if you have questions

### Managing Your Library

**Your Purchases:**
- Access all purchased hooks from your profile
- Re-download anytime (no limits)
- View license details and certificates
- Export license certificates as PDF

**Playlists:**
- Create custom playlists to organize hooks
- Add purchased and favorited hooks
- Share playlists with collaborators
- Make playlists public or private

## For Sellers: Uploading & Selling Hooks

### Becoming a Seller

**Setup Your Seller Account:**
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click "Become a Seller"
3. Connect your Stripe account for payouts
4. Complete seller verification
5. Set your payout preferences

**Stripe Connect Setup:**
- Provide business/individual information
- Add bank account for payouts
- Verify your identity (required by Stripe)
- Set payout schedule (daily, weekly, monthly)

### Uploading Your First Hook

**Upload Process:**
1. Click "Upload" in navigation
2. Drag & drop your audio file (WAV/MP3)
3. Fill in details:
   - Title and description
   - Genre, BPM, key
   - Tags for discoverability
4. Set pricing for each license type
5. Upload cover art (optional but recommended)
6. Click "Publish"

**Best Practices:**
- Use high-quality audio files (WAV preferred)
- Write detailed, honest descriptions
- Add relevant tags for search
- Set competitive pricing
- Create eye-catching cover art
- Preview before publishing

### Managing Your Hooks

**Hook Dashboard:**
- View all your uploaded hooks
- See sales and earnings per hook
- Edit details or pricing anytime
- Pause sales temporarily
- Remove hooks from marketplace

**Version Control:**
- Upload multiple versions of the same hook
- Let buyers choose their preferred version
- Bundle versions for discounts
- Track which versions sell best

**Bundles:**
- Create bundles of related hooks
- Offer discounted bundle pricing
- Promote bundles to increase sales
- Track bundle performance

### Understanding Licenses

**License Types You Can Offer:**

1. **Basic License** ($10-50 typical)
   - Non-commercial use only
   - Personal projects, demos
   - No streaming revenue allowed
   - Attribution required

2. **Premium License** ($50-200 typical)
   - Commercial use allowed
   - Unlimited streams/downloads
   - Music videos, albums, singles
   - Attribution optional

3. **Exclusive License** ($500-5000+ typical)
   - Full ownership transfer
   - Hook removed from marketplace
   - No other sales allowed
   - Complete creative control

**Setting Prices:**
- Research similar hooks in your genre
- Start competitive, adjust based on demand
- Offer bundle discounts
- Consider your experience level

### Getting Paid

**Payout Schedule:**
- Earnings available after 7-day hold period
- Automatic payouts to your Stripe account
- Choose payout frequency in settings
- View detailed earnings reports

**Platform Fees:**
- HookDrop takes 15% platform fee
- Stripe processing fee (~3%)
- You receive ~82% of sale price
- Exclusive licenses have lower fees (10%)

**Multi-Currency:**
- Sell in multiple currencies
- Automatic currency conversion
- Exchange rates updated daily
- Choose your preferred payout currency
