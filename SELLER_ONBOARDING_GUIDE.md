## Getting Started as a Seller

### Step 1: Complete Your Profile

**Profile Optimization:**
- Upload a professional profile picture
- Write a compelling bio (150-300 characters)
- Highlight your music style and experience
- Add links to your social media and portfolio
- Showcase your best work in featured playlists

**Why This Matters:**
Buyers want to know who they're buying from. A complete profile builds trust and increases sales.

### Step 2: Connect Stripe for Payouts

**Stripe Connect Setup:**
1. Click "Become a Seller" in your profile
2. Click "Connect Stripe Account"
3. Choose account type:
   - **Individual**: For solo producers
   - **Business**: For companies/labels
4. Provide required information:
   - Legal name and address
   - Tax ID (SSN or EIN)
   - Bank account details
5. Verify your identity (photo ID required)
6. Set payout schedule (daily, weekly, monthly)

**Verification Timeline:**
- Instant for most users
- Up to 48 hours for manual review
- You can upload hooks immediately, but payouts require verification

### Step 3: Set Your Pricing Strategy

**Understanding License Types:**

**Basic License** - Entry Level
- Best for: Demos, practice, non-commercial use
- Typical price: $10-$50
- What buyers get: Download, personal use only
- Your cut: ~82% after fees

**Premium License** - Commercial Use
- Best for: Released music, streaming, videos
- Typical price: $50-$200
- What buyers get: Commercial rights, unlimited streams
- Your cut: ~82% after fees

**Exclusive License** - Full Rights
- Best for: Serious artists, major projects
- Typical price: $500-$5,000+
- What buyers get: Full ownership, hook removed from marketplace
- Your cut: ~85% after fees (lower platform fee)

**Pricing Tips:**
- Research competitors in your genre
- Start lower to build reputation
- Increase prices as you gain reviews
- Offer bundle discounts
- Consider your production time and costs

## Uploading Your First Hook

### Preparing Your Audio

**Technical Requirements:**
- Format: WAV (preferred) or MP3
- Sample rate: 44.1kHz or higher
- Bit depth: 16-bit minimum, 24-bit preferred
- Length: 15 seconds to 2 minutes
- File size: Under 50MB

**Quality Checklist:**
- No clipping or distortion
- Proper levels (-6dB to -3dB peak)
- Clean mix (no unwanted noise)
- Professional mastering
- Clear, catchy hook

### Upload Process

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Click "Upload" in navigation
2. Drag & drop your audio file
3. Wait for upload and processing
4. Fill in metadata:

**Required Information:**
- Title (clear, descriptive)
- Description (what makes it unique?)
- Genre (select primary genre)
- BPM (exact tempo)
- Key (musical key)
- Tags (5-10 relevant keywords)

**Optional but Recommended:**
- Cover art (1400x1400px minimum)
- Mood/vibe description
- Instruments used
- Inspiration or story behind it
- Similar artists/styles

**Pricing Setup:**
- Set price for each license type
- Enable/disable license types as needed
- Consider bundle pricing
- Preview before publishing

### Writing Great Descriptions

**Do:**
- Be specific about the sound and style
- Mention instruments and production techniques
- Describe the mood and energy
- Suggest use cases (workout music, chill vibes, etc.)
- Be honest about what it is

**Don't:**
- Oversell or make false claims
- Use misleading tags
- Copy descriptions from other sellers
- Include contact info (use platform messaging)
- Guarantee chart success or viral hits

**Example Description:**
```
"Dark trap hook with heavy 808s and eerie piano melody. 
140 BPM in E minor. Perfect for moody rap vocals or drill beats. 
Inspired by Metro Boomin and Southside. Mixed and mastered, 
ready to drop your vocals on top."
```

## Maximizing Your Sales

### Optimization Strategies

**1. Consistent Uploads**
- Upload at least 1-2 hooks per week
- Build a diverse catalog
- Quality over quantity always
- Create upload schedule and stick to it

**2. Tagging & Discoverability**
- Use all available tag slots
- Mix broad and specific tags
- Include genre, mood, and style tags
- Update tags based on search trends

**3. Cover Art Matters**
- Professional, eye-catching design
- Consistent branding across uploads
- High resolution (1400x1400px minimum)
- Avoid text-heavy designs
- Make it stand out in thumbnails

**4. Competitive Pricing**
- Research similar hooks weekly
- Adjust prices based on demand
- Offer introductory pricing for new releases
- Bundle related hooks for discounts

**5. Build Your Brand**
- Develop a signature sound
- Consistent quality across uploads
- Engage with buyers in messages
- Promote on social media
- Collaborate with other producers

### Marketing Your Hooks

**On Platform:**
- Create themed playlists of your work
- Engage with the community
- Respond to all messages promptly
- Leave thoughtful comments on others' work
- Update your profile regularly

**Off Platform:**
- Share on Instagram, Twitter, TikTok
- Create YouTube beat videos
- Email your mailing list
- Collaborate with artists
- Join producer communities
- Offer exclusive deals to followers

**Content Ideas:**
- Behind-the-scenes production videos
- Before/after mixing examples
- Free sample packs (drive traffic)
- Producer tips and tutorials
- Showcase songs made with your hooks

- How many people discovered your hook

- Peak listening times

**Conversion Rate:**

**Revenue:**
- Total earnings by hook

- Bundle performance

- Add more relevant tags
- Create better cover art
- Share more on social media
- Upload more consistently

**If Conversion Is Low:**

- Diversify your catalog

### Free Tier
- Upload up to 5 hooks
- 15% platform fee
- Basic analytics
- Standard support
- **Best for:** Testing the platform

### Pro Tier ($9.99/month)
- Upload up to 50 hooks
- 12% platform fee (save 3%!)
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Featured placement opportunities
- **Best for:** Serious hobbyists

### Premium Tier ($29.99/month)
- Unlimited uploads
- 10% platform fee (save 5%!)
- Comprehensive analytics
- Dedicated support
- Guaranteed featured placement
- Early access to features
- Custom branding
- **Best for:** Professional producers

**ROI Example:**
If you sell $500/month:
- Free: $425 after fees (15%)
- Pro: $430 after fees + $10 = $420 (break even)
- Premium: $450 after fees - $30 = $420 (break even)

At $1000/month in sales, Premium tier saves you $20/month!

## Legal & Licensing

### What You're Selling

**Basic License:**
- Non-exclusive rights
- Personal/non-commercial use
- Attribution required
- No streaming revenue allowed

**Premium License:**
- Non-exclusive commercial rights
- Unlimited distribution
- Streaming revenue allowed
- Attribution optional

**Exclusive License:**
- Full ownership transfer
- Hook removed from marketplace
- All future rights transferred
- One-time sale only

### Your Rights

**You Retain:**
- Copyright of your original work
- Right to create similar hooks
- Credit as the original creator
- Ability to showcase in portfolio

**You Grant:**
- License as specified in sale
- Right for HookDrop to display/promote
- Distribution rights to buyer
- Usage rights per license type

### Protecting Yourself

**Always:**
- Register copyrights for valuable work
- Keep original project files
- Document your creative process
- Read and understand license terms
- Report unauthorized use

**Never:**
- Sell content you don't own
- Sample without clearance
- Misrepresent license terms
- Harass buyers over usage
- Violate platform terms
